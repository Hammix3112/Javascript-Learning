Islamabad

Islamabad is the capital of Pakistan and the famous place there is Faisal Mosque which is gifted by Saudi king Shah Faisal.

Delhi

Delhi is the capital of India and the famous place there is Red Fort made in reign of Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan.

Paris
